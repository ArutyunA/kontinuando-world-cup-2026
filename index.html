The Draw Ceremony

Enter the contestants, dim the lights, gather everyone round the screen, and hit the big button. Pools adjust to the player count — everyone gets one team per pool, and any leftover teams (always the lowest-ranked) sit out. Teams are revealed one by one, finishing with the top seeds. No takesies-backsies.


The Squads

The draw is locked in. Equal squads all round — one team from every pool. May the odds be ever in your favour (except yours, obviously).

Leaderboard

Underdog Multiplier scoring — every win, draw and goal counts, and the unlikelier your team, the more it's worth. Tap a row for the full breakdown.

The Pools

Seven players, so all 48 teams are ranked by real bookmaker odds (BetMGM, June 2026) and dealt into 7 pools of 7 — everyone draws one team per pool for an equal squad of seven. The longer the odds, the bigger the points multiplier. The one extra slot is filled by drawing a single bottom-pool team twice, so two players share it (🔁) and both score its points — no team sits out.

Matches & Results

The Sacred Rules

Scoring system: The Underdog Multiplier. Everything your six teams do earns points — multiplied by how unfancied they are. A Haiti goal is worth six Spain goals. Choose your loyalties accordingly.

⚽ Match points (per team)

  • Win: 3 pts
  • Draw: 1 pt
  • Every goal scored: 1 pt
  • Clean sheet (no goals conceded): 2 pts
  • Winning a penalty shootout counts as a win — but shootout goals don't score (we're not animals).

🚀 Progress bonuses

  • Reach the Round of 32: +5
  • Reach the Round of 16: +5
  • Reach the Quarter-finals: +5
  • Reach the Semi-finals: +5
  • Reach the Final: +5
  • Win the World Cup: +10 👑
  • Win the third-place match: +3 (the bronze of honour)

✖️ The Multiplier

All of the above gets multiplied by your team's seed band (fixed by odds ranking, whatever the player count):

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Any number of players

  • With N players, each pool holds N consecutive seeds and everyone draws one team per pool — equal squads, always.
  • If 48 doesn't divide evenly, the bottom pool is topped up by drawing a random lowest-ranked team twice, so a pair of players share that team (🔁) — both score its points. No team sits out.
  • (If too many duplicates would be needed, the lowest-ranked teams sit out instead and score for nobody.)
  • Changing the players after the draw means a redraw — reset the draw first.
  • Resetting (or importing over) a locked draw needs the organiser password 🔒 — no "accidental" redraws when your Giants flop.

🧮 Worked examples

  • Spain win 2–0: (3 win + 2 goals + 2 clean sheet) × 1 = 7 pts
  • Haiti win 2–1: (3 + 2) × 5 = 25 pts 🎉
  • Scotland draw 1–1: (1 + 1) × 3 = 6 pts
  • Curaçao sneak into the Round of 16: bonuses alone = (5 + 5) × 5 = 50 pts of pure chaos

👑 Winning the sweepstake

  • Most points after the Final wins the family trophy and four years of unbearable smugness — no rematch until 2030.
  • Tiebreak 1: whoever owns the team that went furthest in the tournament.
  • Tiebreak 2: most total goals scored by your teams.

📡 How scores update

  • While a match is being played the page refreshes itself about every 20 seconds, so the leaderboard tracks goals close to live.
  • Between matches it idles, then picks straight back up at the next kick-off — completely hands-off.
  • Nothing to press: just open the page and the latest scores load themselves.
